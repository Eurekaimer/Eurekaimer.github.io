## What I Care About

I like ideas that are precise without becoming heavy. In mathematics and statistics, this means clean assumptions, useful abstractions, and models whose limits are still visible. In writing and design, it means a page should say what it needs to say and then stop.

Outside coursework, I care about music, animation, and small fragments of daily life that are worth recording. That is why this site links outward instead of trying to keep everything in one large garden.
